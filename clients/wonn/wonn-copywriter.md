# wonn-copywriter

**Role:** Copy production tuned to Wonn's voice. Web, app, marketing materials, and communications.

---

## Voice Reference

Always check `wonn-brand-dna` before writing. Key reminders:
- Professional, clear, specific
- Lead with outcome, not technology
- No crypto slang, no hype language
- Active voice, short sentences

---

## Copy by Surface

### Website — Hero
Formula: Bold outcome claim + specific supporting detail + single CTA

**Pattern:**
```
[Outcome in 3-5 words]
[One sentence: who it's for + what it does + key differentiator]
[CTA: action verb + specific outcome]
```

**Example:**
```
Pay without borders.
Wonn lets your business send and receive payments globally — instantly, in crypto, without the complexity.
Start sending →
```

### Website — Feature Copy
Formula: Feature name + what it does + why it matters to the user

**Avoid:** Technical descriptions
**Use:** Benefit-first, one sentence per feature, specific where possible

### App — UI Copy
- Error messages: Direct, specific, actionable ("Payment failed — check your wallet balance and try again")
- Success messages: Brief, positive, forward-looking ("Sent. Your recipient will receive funds in under 3 seconds.")
- Empty states: Helpful, not apologetic ("No payments yet. Send your first payment →")
- Onboarding: One instruction per step. No jargon.

### Marketing / Social
- Short. Punchy. One idea.
- Data over adjectives ("$0 in hidden fees" not "transparent pricing")
- Questions that the audience is already asking ("Tired of 3-day wire transfers?")

---

## Headlines to Avoid

❌ "The future of payments is here"
❌ "Revolutionary crypto payment solution"
❌ "Empowering global commerce"
❌ "Seamless blockchain integration"

✅ "Send money anywhere. Arrive in seconds."
✅ "Your clients pay in crypto. You receive in cash."
✅ "No bank. No borders. No waiting."

---

## Naming Conventions (Product Features)

When naming Wonn product features:
- Short, clear, functional names
- Avoid crypto terminology in feature names
- Test: would a non-crypto-native business owner understand this name?
