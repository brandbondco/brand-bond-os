# wonn-social

**Role:** Platform strategy, content calendar, and social media production for Wonn.

---

## Platform Strategy

### Primary Platforms

| Platform | Role | Content type | Frequency |
|---|---|---|---|
| **LinkedIn** | B2B credibility, thought leadership | Business insights, product updates, case studies | 3-4x/week |
| **X (Twitter)** | Community, real-time, tech audience | Short takes, product news, engagement | Daily |
| **Instagram** | Brand presence, visual storytelling | Design-forward visuals, culture | 2-3x/week |

### Not a priority (yet):
- TikTok — audience skews too young/casual for B2B positioning
- Facebook — low ROI for B2B fintech in target markets

---

## Content Pillars

| Pillar | % of content | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| **Product** | 30% | Shows features and benefits in real scenarios |
| **Education** | 25% | Builds authority, answers real business questions |
| **Social proof** | 20% | Customer stories, data, use cases |
| **Brand / culture** | 15% | Humanizes the company |
| **Engagement** | 10% | Questions, polls, conversation starters |

Avoid: Content that looks like it's from a crypto trading account. Always business-first.

---

## Post Formats

### LinkedIn
- Hook line (first 2 lines must stop the scroll)
- Body: 3-5 short paragraphs or structured list
- No hashtag overload (max 3, relevant only)
- CTA: comment, share, or link — one per post

### X
- Under 280 characters for primary message
- Threads for longer topics (number them: 1/, 2/, etc.)
- Engage with fintech and business accounts — not crypto speculation

### Instagram
- Visual first — copy supports the visual
- Caption: short, conversational, on-brand
- Stories: product features, behind the scenes, quick tips

---

## Content Calendar Framework

**Weekly rhythm:**
```
Monday    → Product or feature highlight
Wednesday → Education / insight
Friday    → Social proof or brand
```

**Monthly anchor content:**
- Product update or launch
- Customer story
- Market insight or data post
