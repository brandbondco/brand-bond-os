# wonn-radar

**Role:** Competitor monitoring and trend tracking in the crypto payment sector.

---

## Monitoring Scope

### Competitors to Watch

| Company | Why monitor |
|---|---|
| **BitPay** | Category leader — any move signals market direction |
| **Coinbase Commerce** | Exchange-backed — threat if they simplify UX |
| **Binance Pay** | Volume threat — any B2B push is significant |
| **Strike** | Lightning network — if they expand globally |
| **Stripe** (crypto features) | If Stripe enters crypto payments seriously, category changes |

### Signals to Track

**Product signals:**
- New features launched
- Pricing changes
- UX/design updates
- New integrations or partnerships

**Market signals:**
- Regulatory changes in UAE, EU, US
- New crypto payment entrants
- Traditional fintech adding crypto features
- Exchange rate and adoption data

**Brand signals:**
- Messaging changes (what are they now claiming?)
- Campaign launches
- PR and press coverage

---

## Radar Report Format

When reporting competitor or market intelligence:

```
SIGNAL          — What happened
SOURCE          — Where observed (with date)
RELEVANCE       — Why this matters for Wonn (1-2 sentences)
RECOMMENDED ACTION — What Wonn should consider doing (if anything)
```

Not every signal requires action. The job is to ensure no significant move goes unnoticed.

---

## Reporting Cadence

- **Weekly:** Quick scan — any significant product or messaging changes
- **Monthly:** Full competitive landscape review
- **Immediate:** Regulatory changes or major competitor launches
